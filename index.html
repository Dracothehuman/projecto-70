Middle School

The middle school building is where the year 7-8 students are located

The building has 8 main general classrooms that the students are allocated to learn in. There is also a practical classroom for science experiments that the students will take part in. There is an art room where the students to the art class in aswell.

Out door gym

A teaching and playing space for all the students.

The outdoor gym (Gym 2) is a place where the students go to if their teacher has assigned them that spot for them to learn about the sport that they are learning about. But, it is more commonly known for being used by the students at lunch and reccess to play their favorite sports.
